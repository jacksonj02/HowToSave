Investing in Real Estate

Investing in real estate is a great way to generate passive income. There are multiple ways to make money in real estate. The main factors that go into buying real estate are a down payment and credit. We will talk mainly about a down payment. A down payment is a percentage of the total price put down to start the owning process of the house. Down payments can be as low as 3.5% with a FHA loan and on average around 20% with a conventional loan.
