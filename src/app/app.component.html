How To Save

Get a source of income.
Possibly more than 1 depending on how fast you want to save

Open a high yield savings account.
A good one is American Express which gives a return of 3% a year and compounds monthly.

Example: $1,000 in this savings account will make around $30 a year or $2.50 a month.

Put money in your savings account every week, paycheck, or month consistently.

Example: Put $100 in a savings account every week and in a year you have around $5,000 + 3% interest

Be as consistent as you can.

If you fall off, start again.

Next Steps

Tell friends and family to save. Working with a team will help you by keeping each other motivated to save and then you can also put your money together to invest.
